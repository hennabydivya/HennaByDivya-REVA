  HOW TO UPDATE YOUR ABOUT PAGE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

This folder makes it SUPER EASY to update your About page!

FOLDER STRUCTURE:
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
about-content/
├── portrait.jpg              ← Your portrait photo
├── about-text.txt           ← Your about text
└── HOW-TO-UPDATE-ABOUT-PAGE.txt  ← This file


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
TO UPDATE YOUR PORTRAIT PHOTO:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Take or choose a professional photo of yourself
2. Name it: portrait.jpg (or portrait.png, portrait.jpeg)
3. Drop it in this folder: about-content/
4. Done! The website automatically loads it

RECOMMENDED:
• Size: 800×1000px or larger
• Professional quality
• Good lighting
• Shows you doing henna art (or a professional headshot)


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
TO UPDATE YOUR ABOUT TEXT:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Open: about-text.txt
2. Edit the text (keep it 2-3 paragraphs for best look)
3. Save the file
4. Let me know - I'll update the HTML for you!


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
AFTER MAKING CHANGES:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Open GitHub Desktop
2. You'll see your changed files listed
3. Type a note: "Updated about page"
4. Click "Commit to main"
5. Click "Push origin"
6. Wait 2 minutes - your site updates!


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
TIPS FOR BEST LOOK:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

✓ Keep text concise (2-3 paragraphs)
✓ Use high-quality portrait photo
✓ Compress image before uploading (max 500KB)
✓ Tell your story authentically
✓ Focus on your passion and experience
