Hello bride-to-be!

Thank you so much for your interest in working with me!

With over 15 years of experience as a bridal henna artist, I've come to value something far deeper than just the design itself—I value the process. I pour hours of intentional artistry into each bridal henna, and over time, I've learned that the experience is most fulfilling when it's shared with clients who truly appreciate, understand and are looking for that.

Whether you're drawn to something incredibly simple and minimal or a fully custom, detailed piece, I find the most joy working with brides who are willing to give the art the time it needs, and who see the mehendi session not just as a task—but as a moment of stillness, creativity, and celebration.

For 2026 and beyond, I'll be significantly reducing the number of bridal bookings I take on. Instead, I'm curating each experience with care and only booking with brides where we both feel like we're the right fit—for your style, your energy, and your approach to this special part of your wedding.

If this resonates with you, I'd love to get to know you better. Please take a moment to complete the questions below so we can explore if this feels aligned.
