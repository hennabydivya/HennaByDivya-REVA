How to Use & Care

1

Store

Keep in freezer until ready. Thaw at room temperature for 30 minutes before use.

2

Prepare

Apply to clean, dry skin. Gently exfoliate beforehand for best results.

3

Apply

Create your design. Take your time - this is a meditation in artistry.

4

Wait

Leave on for 4-6 hours (or overnight). The longer, the deeper the stain.

5

Seal

Apply lemon-sugar mixture for a richer, longer-lasting stain.

6

Reveal

Gently scrape off. Avoid water for 12-24 hours. Watch the magic deepen.
